* [cite_start]**View All:** Saare products ki list screen par dekhein[cite: 10].
* [cite_start]**Search:** Kisi bhi product ko search karein[cite: 10].
* [cite_start]**Update:** Product ki details ya price change karein[cite: 10].
* [cite_start]**Delete:** Kisi product ko list se remove karein[cite: 10].
* [cite_start]**Check Stock:** Kisi product ki quantity check karein[cite: 10].
